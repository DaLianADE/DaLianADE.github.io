Opportunities

A shared route into
the group.

DLADE welcomes enquiries across several career stages. Specific funded calls, deadlines and appointment conditions vary, so candidates should confirm current availability before preparing a formal application.

01

Research appointment

Postdoctoral Fellow

For researchers who have completed, or are close to completing, a PhD in mathematics or a closely related field. Projects may be developed around nonlinear PDEs, fluid mechanics, Hamiltonian dynamics or related themes.

  • Research-focused appointment
  • Mentoring and seminar participation
  • Funding and duration subject to the relevant call
02

Graduate research

PhD Researcher

For strong graduates seeking doctoral training in rigorous analysis, differential equations or dynamical systems, with supervision through the School of Mathematical Sciences.

  • Structured doctoral research
  • Group seminars and academic exchange
  • Admission and scholarship rules apply
03

Academic exchange

Visiting Researcher

For faculty members, postdoctoral researchers and doctoral students wishing to pursue a focused collaboration or participate in the group’s academic activities in Dalian.

  • Short or medium-term visits
  • Collaborative research planning
  • Dates and support arranged individually

02 · RESEARCH FIT

Research fit and eligibility

Research themes

  • Analysis and nonlinear partial differential equations
  • Incompressible fluid mechanics, interfaces and waves
  • Hamiltonian dynamical systems and stability
  • Celestial mechanics, symplectic geometry and mathematical physics

What we look for

  • A strong mathematical foundation appropriate to the opportunity
  • Clear research motivation and alignment with at least one group theme
  • Capacity for independent work and constructive collaboration
  • Academic English sufficient for research communication

03 · APPLICATION

How to express interest

  1. 1

    Check research fit

    Identify the group theme and potential academic contact most closely aligned with your work.

  2. 2

    Prepare a concise enquiry

    Introduce your background, proposed topic, preferred timing and the opportunity you are considering.

  3. 3

    Attach core materials

    Include a curriculum vitae, publication list where relevant, research statement and contact details for academic referees.

  4. 4

    Confirm the formal route

    After an initial academic discussion, follow the current DUT appointment, admission or visitor procedure.

Important

This page is an expression-of-interest guide, not a formal vacancy notice. Funding, eligibility, deadlines and required documents must be confirmed for each opportunity.

04 · INTERNATIONAL APPLICANTS

Support before and after arrival

International candidates may ask for clarification on the appropriate application route and documents. Once an appointment or admission is confirmed, the relevant university offices provide the official procedures.

  • Host and invitation documentation
  • Visa and residence-permit process guidance
  • Accommodation and arrival information
  • English-language onboarding and local orientation
