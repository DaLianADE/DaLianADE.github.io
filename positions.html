Open PhD & postdoc positions.

We welcome applications for three PhD and four postdoctoral positions at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Dalian University of Technology. The funding period runs from January 2027 to June 2031, and applications are reviewed on a rolling basis until all positions are filled.

Open positions

Seven openings.
Two research groups.

The positions are based in Dalian, China. Successful applicants will work in the research group of Prof. Xian Liao or Prof. Lei Zhao, with research focused on Analysis, Differential Equations or Dynamical Systems.

PhD Positions

Four-year, research-focused doctoral positions with no teaching obligation. A competitive mathematical background is preferred. PhD enrollment begins from September 2027.

  • Four-year positions
  • No teaching obligation
  • Start from September 2027

Postdoctoral Positions

Two-year, research-focused postdoctoral positions with no teaching obligation and a competitive salary. Postdoctoral appointments can commence from January 2027.

  • Two-year positions
  • No teaching obligation
  • Start from January 2027

PhD terms and application documents

Terms

PhD positions are for four years, are research-focused and carry no teaching obligation. A competitive mathematical background is preferred. Enrollment begins from September 2027.

  • Cover letter

    State your research interests and preferred supervisor.

  • Curriculum vitae

    Include the contact details of two academic referees.

  • Publication list

    Include a list of publications, if any.

  • MSc thesis

    Submit the thesis if available; otherwise, provide a description of the MSc thesis project.

  • Academic records

    Submit university diplomas or certificates and transcripts of records for BSc and MSc studies, if available.

Postdoc terms and application documents

Terms

Postdoc positions are for two years, are research-focused and carry no teaching obligation. A competitive salary is offered. Applicants should be under 32 years old at the time of appointment and have 1-2 publications in internationally recognized journals; arXiv preprints do not count.

  • Cover letter

    State your research interests and preferred supervisor.

  • Curriculum vitae

    Include the contact details of two academic referees.

  • Publication list

    Include your complete list of publications.

  • Research statement

    Provide a research statement of 2-3 pages.

  • PhD certificate

    Submit the certificate if available; otherwise, state the expected date of your defense.

Submit one complete PDF

  • Prepare the required documents

    Use the five-item document list for the position to which you are applying.

  • Combine the application

    Combine all required documents into a single PDF file.

  • Email the group

    Send the single PDF to dalianade(at) foxmail.com.

  • Rolling review

    Applications remain open until all positions are filled.

Review dates

The first committee meeting will evaluate application materials on August 31, 2026. Subsequent reviews will be conducted every three months.
