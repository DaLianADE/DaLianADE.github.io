Research activities

From rigorous analysis
to dynamical structure.

Our research connects nonlinear equations, geometric structures and physical phenomena. We seek rigorous explanations for the behavior of fluids, waves, Hamiltonian systems and celestial motion.

View research themes ↓
01

Partial differential equations

Analysis, PDEs & fluid mechanics

Well-posedness, regularity, singularity formation, free-interface problems and equations arising in fluid dynamics.

02

Dynamical systems

Hamiltonian dynamical systems

Stability, invariant structures, normal forms and qualitative behavior in Hamiltonian and nonlinear systems.

03

Geometry & mathematical physics

Celestial mechanics & symplectic geometry

Geometric structures connecting orbital dynamics, symplectic methods and problems in mathematical physics.
