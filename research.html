Research focus

From equations
to phenomena.

Our research connects nonlinear equations, geometric structures and physical phenomena. We seek rigorous explanations for the behavior of fluids, waves, Hamiltonian systems and celestial motion.

01

Analysis & partial differential equations

Analysis, nonlinear PDEs & fluid mechanics

Well-posedness, regularity, singularity formation, free-interface problems and equations arising in incompressible fluid mechanics.

02

Dynamical systems & geometry

Hamiltonian dynamics, celestial mechanics & symplectic geometry

Stability, invariant structures, normal forms and geometric methods for Hamiltonian systems, orbital dynamics and problems in celestial mechanics.
